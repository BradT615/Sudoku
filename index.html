Instructions

  • Digits from 1 to 9: Sudoku is played with the numbers 1 to 9.
  • No repetition in rows: Each row of the grid must contain each of the digits from 1 to 9 exactly once.
  • No repetition in columns: Each column of the grid must contain each of the digits from 1 to 9 exactly once.
  • No repetition in boxes: Each of the 3x3 boxes must contain each of the digits from 1 to 9 exactly once.
  • Pre-filled squares: Some squares will be filled in to start the game; these numbers cannot be changed.
